<span data-ttu-id="74aec-117">Eis como funciona a solução:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="74aec-117">Here's how the solution works:</span></span>

7. <span data-ttu-id="74aec-124">As mensagens do IoT Hub são agregadas no Time Series Insights.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="74aec-124">Messages from IoT Hub are aggregated in Time Series Insights.</span></span>
